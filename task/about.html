ABOUT US

Established in the year 1979, Hotel Aida is one of the first mainstream hotels in Kottayam. The hotel is conveniently located on SH-1 (MC Road) and the hotel building is a landmark in Kottayam. The hotel has evolved over the decades and adapted to the fast changing trends in hospitality. The hotel has a distinct character that blends tradition with modernity. The hotel boasts of 36 Guest Rooms, 3 Banquet Halls, a splendid 150 seater multicuisine Restaurant, a chic Restobar and two themed Bars. The hotel is 3 Star classified by the Ministry of Tourism, India and has been awarded the “Certificate of Excellence” by TripAdvisor in the years 2014, 2015 and 2020. Over the past four decades, the hotel has been delighting guests with an unparalleled touch of hospitality!
